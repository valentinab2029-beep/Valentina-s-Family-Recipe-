My Family's Famous Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet time!

About this recipe

This red velvet cake is soft, moist, and just the perfect amount of chocolate. It is perfect for birthdays, parties, gatherings with friends and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • Red food coloring

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Combine both mixtures.
  5. Pour the batter into a baking pan.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool and add any frosting you would like.

Why is this recipe so special to me :

This recipe has been in my family for decades and is very special to me. We make it for birthdays and important celebrations, and it always brings everyone together. It is more than just a dessert, it is a tradition that reminds me of family and happy memories.
